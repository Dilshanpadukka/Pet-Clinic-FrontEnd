About US

At PetMedCare, we have a 25 year history of connecting, caring and curing your companion animals.

Bound by an organizational culture - one that fosters respect and compassion to all animals and their human caretakers - PetMedCare sets itself apart from other veterinary services in the country by treating your companion animals with the kind of care, love and kindness we show towards our own fur babies.

Because pets are family. At PetMedCare, we understand this truth. At PetMedCare, we wholeheartedly agree.
